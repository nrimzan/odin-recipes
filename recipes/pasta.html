Description

Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine, made from wheat flour and water, and comes in various shapes and sizes. It can be served with a variety of sauces and ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) to the skillet. Stir in the dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine with the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious pasta dish!
