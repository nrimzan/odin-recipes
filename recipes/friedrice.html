Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Fried rice is a popular dish made with cooked rice, vegetables, and often meat or seafood, stir-fried together with soy sauce and other seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until they are tender.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the beaten eggs to the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir everything together.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, mixing well to ensure all ingredients are coated.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped green onions.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade fried rice!
